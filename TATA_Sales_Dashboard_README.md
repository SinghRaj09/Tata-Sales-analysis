
# 📊 TATA Sales Dashboard – Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive **Power BI dashboard** built using TATA retail sales data. The dashboard helps analyze **sales performance, customer behavior, and product trends** across different countries and time periods.

The goal of this project is to provide **business insights through data visualization** to help stakeholders make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall **sales performance**
- Identify **top performing countries**
- Understand **best-selling products**
- Track **monthly sales trends**
- Monitor **customer and order growth**

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- **Power BI** – Dashboard creation & visualization  
- **Microsoft Excel / CSV** – Data source  
- **Power Query** – Data cleaning and transformation  
- **DAX** – Data modeling and calculations  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 1️⃣ Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
The dashboard highlights three main business metrics:

- **Total Sales:** $9.84M  
- **Total Orders:** 18.40K  
- **Total Customers:** 4K  

These KPIs provide a quick overview of overall business performance.

---

### 2️⃣ Sales by Country
This visualization shows **total revenue generated by each country**.

**Key Insight**
- **United Kingdom contributes the highest sales (~$8.3M)**.
- Other countries such as Netherlands, Ireland, Germany, and France contribute smaller portions.

This indicates the business is **highly dependent on the UK market**.

---

### 3️⃣ Top Selling Products
The **Total Sales by Description** chart highlights the best-performing products.

Top products include:

- DOTCOM POSTAGE  
- PAPER CRAFT, LITTLE BIRDIE  
- REGENCY CAKESTAND 3 TIER  
- PARTY BUNTING  
- WHITE HANGING HEART T-LIGHT HOLDER  

These products generate the **highest revenue contribution**.

---

### 4️⃣ Sales Trend Over Time
The **monthly sales trend** shows how revenue changes throughout the year.

**Insights**
- Sales gradually increase from **September to November**
- **November shows the highest sales (~$1.51M)**  
- Sales drop significantly in **December**

This indicates strong **seasonal sales patterns**, possibly linked to **holiday shopping periods**.

---

## 🔎 Filters / Slicers
The dashboard includes interactive filters:

- **Country Filter** – Analyze sales by specific country  
- **Month Filter** – View trends for selected months  

These filters allow users to **dynamically explore the data**.

---

## 📈 Key Business Insights
- The **United Kingdom dominates sales**, contributing the majority of revenue.
- A few products generate a **large portion of total sales**.
- Sales increase significantly in the **last quarter of the year**.
- Seasonal trends suggest strong **holiday-driven demand**.
